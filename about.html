Virée

Get to know us

Virée is a french word that means trip, much like the more common journée it can be used to describe a tour, trip or excursion. Here at Virée we love travelling the world. Taking solo trips to different countries and experiencing different cultures and cuisines.
However, travelling alone can get lonely, that's were we come in. Connecting you to other travelers who are looking to meet new people while experiencing the world out there. If you are just looking for someone to join you on a hike, or to a new restaurant you would like to try, feel free to post about your trip and connect with the multitude of others who have similar interests to you.
